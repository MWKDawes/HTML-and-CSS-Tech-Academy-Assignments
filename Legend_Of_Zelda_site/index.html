BREADTH OF THE WILD

Breath of the Wild does what no other can

The game Breath of the Wild is an almalgamation of fourty years worth of ground-breaking videogames and fantastic story-telling and not only combines the hard work done, but combines the worlds the games take place in.

The First World

The definitive first world in Legend Of Zelda takes place within the games of Skyward Sword, The Minish Cap, Four Swords, and Ocarina of time. This continues until a time traveled Link's first showdown with Ganandorf in which this world is split into three distinct timelines: the timeline that Link left to travel to the future and fight Ganandorf but then subsequently returns to, the future timeline that Link leaves after defeating Ganandorf, and a timeline separate from these where Link dies fighting Ganandorf

Link returns to his timeline

Link defeats Ganondorf and is returned to his own past, where he is able to warn of the threat before it arises. This timeline spans the games Majora's Mask, Twilight Princess, and Four Swords Adventures. This timeline introduces the Twilight realm and other dark versions of the kingdom of Hyrule.

The timelink Link left after defeating Ganondorf

This timeline spans the games The Wind Waker, Phantom Tracks, and Spirit Tracks. Ganondorf's defeat merely bought time for the future timeline. A while after being sealed by the original "Hero of Time" Link, Ganondorf breaks free. This causes the goddesses of Hyrule to drown the kingdom to the bottom of an ocean and seal him away forever. Or...at least until a new hero can come along.

Link is defeated

This timeline spans the games of A Link to The Past, Oracle of Ages/Seasons, Link's Awakening, The Legend of Zelda, and The Adventure of Link. This timeline follows what happens if Link is defeated and Ganondorf triumphant in obtaining the Triforce. Ganondorf uses his newfound power to wipe out the majority of Hyrule's forces and even the royal bloodline, and floods the land with monsters. In a last ditch effort the Kingdom is able to push Ganondorf back into the Dark Realm he retrieved the Triforce from and imprison him there. Until a dark wizard came to try and set him free...

The significance of the Breath of the Wild world

There's much proof to tell us that Breath of the Wild/Tears of the Kingdom are conjoinments of the three timelines that this epic fantasy tale has drawn from. Not only are there artifacts and people that are involved in their own timeline, there are even fossils of ancient Gods that you meet along the way in the different games.
